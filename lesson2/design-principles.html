Fitt's Law

Newegg

newegg.com

One thing I liked about newegg.com on mobile is that the ADD TO CART button doesn't move while you scroll. They made it easier to add items to carts by putting it in a place that is easy to access. Fitt's Law in action.

White Space and Clean Design

Expert Vagabond Photography

expertvagabond.com

I found Expert Vagabond almost on accident. I liked their use of white space to separate content. I also noticed that they use differnt shades of white for the same purpose.

Contrast

Lego

lego.com

Lego uses contrast to the extreme. They use contrasting colors to differentiate the "Play Zone" section from the "Explore" section. They also use different style buttons to ensure you go where you want to.
