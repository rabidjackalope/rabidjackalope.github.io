Personas

1. This web site is designed for a casual parusal of weather events by those who want to know what's happening.

2. This website is designed for a closer examination of the weather patterns to determine how to plan your day.

Scenarios

1. A middle-schooler likes to view maps of the current weather conditions.

2. A truck-driver is heading out to one of the towns and wants to know if a jacket is required.

3. The local storm chasers need a quick analysis of the current conditions to determine where to travel.

4. Mother's with children who want to send their childern outside before their sanity leaves.

5. Sports enthusiasts who need accurate weather information to determine if their will be playing.
